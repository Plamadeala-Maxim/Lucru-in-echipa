THE OUTER LAIR OF SKIN.

The skin’s main layers include the epidermis,
dermis and hypodermis and is prone to many problems,
including skin cancer, acne, wrinkles and rashes.
Your skin, along with your hair, nails, oil glands
and sweat glands, is part of the integumentary system.
“Integumentary” means a body’s outer covering.
